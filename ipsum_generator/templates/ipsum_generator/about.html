About Cricket Ipsum

Come on, own up. Who's responsible for this?

This website was created as a learning exercise by Rich Cairns. What happened was, my friend Craig showed me the excellent Hipster Ipsum, and I thought it would be fun to build something similar.

OK, but why cricket?

I wanted a source of funny words. I don't know much about cricket, but I do know that it has a unique and rich vocabulary... so here we are. Most of the words on the list were found on Google. I don't know what they all mean.

It's a bit bare bones...

Yep, although I prefer "work in progress". Still to do, in no particular order:

  • Allow the user to choose how many paragraphs of placeholder text they want.
  • Add some logging to see how much the site gets used.
  • Make the styling a bit more exciting.
  • Add more crickety phrases to randomly select from.
  • Provide placeholder text over an API.
  • Add some sort of header image. If anyone has an open-source-licenced old-timey picture of a cricket match (I'm thinking "huge moustaches" here) then I'd love to get a copy.
